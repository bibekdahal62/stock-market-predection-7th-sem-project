The Project

Why we built this

If you've ever tried to invest in Nepal's stock market, you'll know the frustration — most tools stop at showing you yesterday's charts. There's very little that actually tries to look ahead, especially for ordinary investors who don't have the time to analyse hundreds of data points manually.

That's the gap we wanted to close. Hamro Stock applies two machine learning models — LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory networks) and Random Forest — to historical NEPSE stock data and produces short-term price trend forecasts in a form that's easy to read and act on.

It's built for students, curious investors, and anyone who wants to understand the market a little better. We're very clear that this is an educational tool — the predictions are not financial advice — but we do believe data-driven thinking leads to better decisions.
